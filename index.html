Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our forex trading services

What is Blith Imperial Trading Co. Ltd?

Blith Imperial Trading Co. Ltd is a premier forex trading company that provides innovative trading solutions, allowing investors to thrive alongside seasoned professionals. We offer both copy trading services and proprietary trading strategies to help clients achieve their financial goals.

How much do I need to start investing?

Our minimum investment amount is $5,000. This allows us to properly manage your account while implementing effective risk management strategies. Higher investment amounts may qualify for premium features and personalized account management.

What are your fees?

We charge a performance fee of 25% on profits generated. This means we only get paid when you profit, aligning our interests with yours. There are no hidden fees or management charges regardless of performance.

How do you achieve 25% monthly returns?

Our team of experienced traders utilizes a combination of technical analysis, fundamental analysis, and proprietary algorithms to identify high-probability trading opportunities. We employ strict risk management protocols to protect capital while maximizing returns. Past performance is not indicative of future results, but we have consistently achieved this target through our disciplined approach.

How often can I withdraw my profits?

Profit withdrawals are processed monthly. You can request a withdrawal at the end of each trading month after performance fees have been calculated and deducted. Withdrawal requests typically take 3-5 business days to process.

Is my investment secure?

We prioritize the security of your investment through several measures:
1. We work exclusively with regulated brokers
2. Client funds are held in segregated accounts
3. We employ robust risk management strategies
4. Regular performance reporting and transparency
While all trading involves risk, we take every precaution to protect your capital.

What is your risk management strategy?

Our risk management strategy includes:
1. Never risking more than 2% of capital on a single trade
2. Using stop-loss orders on all positions
3. Diversifying across currency pairs and trading strategies
4. Continuous monitoring of market conditions
5. Regular strategy reviews and adjustments

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in complete transparency. Our only fee is the 25% performance fee on profits. There are no setup fees, management fees, or hidden charges. The performance fee is only applied when we generate profits for you.
